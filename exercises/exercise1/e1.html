Exercise 1: Self-Portrait



Self Portrait: Live

My name is Teodora, I'm gonna be starting my third year as an Integrated Media Expanded Animation student in the fall. I decided to use blob mixer to create my self-portrait because it best captures how I feel about myself: an abstract body. I used desaturated colours and slowed down the speed to match how quiet and tired I usually am. The background and blob are similar in tone to reflect my desire to blend into the background. For the same reason, I removed all the gloss (clearcoat). In contrast, the orange peering from below represents my emotions and passions, always daring to rise to the surface without delay. Lastly, some purely aesthetic choices I made include not adding a title, and the blob taking on a flower-like form.
